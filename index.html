PRISCILLA SAMUEL NADAR IN SOCIAL WORK

Being an advocate it was always taught justice has to be rendered to poor and needy, I being a women I wanted to work for women and on their problems. Women are said they are empowered and they are taking position/ designation from politics to corporate but I always felt it is only for few women and the rest of the women are still under clutches of social menace like Dowry, Dowry death, Prostitution, Child marriage, female infanticide, rape, molestation, no proper education, no equal rights provided as compared to male.

Since various NGO is working on issues of Sex worker, Human Trafficking, helping / assisting rape victim and also working on various other social cause I thought of taking different social Cause which was untouched. That was “DOWRY ISSUE”

According to me once the women get married and if husband demand dowry then the NGO / Judiciary comes to rescue women, but there is one period in women life where dowry is demanded highhandedly by in-law and girl parents/ relative has to succumb to dowry demand just because the girl has to get married. India has lot of case pending in court where women has been killed just because they were not able to meet dowry demand. And sometime parent lose their entire life time earning just to get one girl child married by paying dowry.

In India because of Dowry, female feticide also increased by time. Because once girl child is born parents of a girl child feel burdened hence they kill girl child by aborting or by any other mean and even if girl child is born she is not treated at par with boy child.

However to fight this menace study was done as to why dowry has increased in India and what are the steps taken by government to stop this social menace called dowry. After the thorough study it was realized that though Government enacted a law called “DOWRY PROHIBITION ACT” 1961 and it is not been implement in its spirit.

In Dowry Prohibition Act as per section 8(b) the State Government has to appoint Dowry Prohibition officer at required. And the Dowry Prohibition Officer can be appointed at District Level, Taluka Level, etc. However the state government till 2013 not appointed single Dowry Prohibition officer, and even after the Honorable High Court passed an Order to appoint the Dowry Prohibition officer till date the government never initiated action to appoint Dowry Prohibition Officer, and if the government would have implemented this Act and Dowry Prohibition Officer would have taken an action strictly as per the Dowry Prohibition Act, today Indian women would have attained the epitome in their personal and professional life. And while researching on the Dowry it was realized that the Marriage broker, online matrimonial website, Matrimonial Coloumn in newspaper are also the responsible for the increasing demand in DOWRY.

Hence the petition was filed in the Bombay High Court bearing No- 52 of 2013. I filed this petition as Petitioner in Person and prayed as followed:

Strict implementation of Dowry Prohibition Act Petition was filed to implement Dowry Prohibition Act strictly and to take action against the people who violate the provision of Dowry Prohibition Act.

ORDERS OBTAINED FROM HIGH COURT

APPOINTMENT OF DOWRY PROHIBITION OFFICER AND TO CONSTITUTE ADVISORY BOARD
  • To appoint as many Dowry Prohibition Officer as required at District Level, Taluka level etc.
  • To constitute advisory board for the purpose of advising and assisting the Dowry Prohibition Officers in the efficient performance of their functions under this Act. Accordingly State Government appointed an advisory board as per the Provision of Dowry Prohibition Act.

REGULARIZATION OF MARRIAGE BUREAU

In our country the Marriage bureau is one of the reason for the increase in the dowry demand and Marriage bureau was operating business without any registration. Marriage broker increased the dowry demand in their market so that there is a hike in their commission. With a passage of time, poor and educated women were not able to get married.

Because the marriage broker/marriage bureau were controlling the destiny of young men and women and they became the decision maker as to who has to marry whom. Since the Marriage Bureau was doing business without registration there was no control in the Marriage Bureau by the Government.

Since this point was argued in the Court the Honorable Bombay High Court passed an Order that the Marriage Bureau has to be registered under the Maharashtra Regulation of Marriage Bureaus and Registration of Marriages Act, 1998 . Prior to the 2013 only 15-20 marriage bureau was registered but after the High Court passed an Order for registration of Marriage Bureau more than 600 marriage bureau got registered by 2017.

CLEANING OF GARBAGE AND PROTECTING ENVIRONMENT IN AAREY POWAI ROAD

CLEANING OF GARBAGE AND PROTECTING ENVIRONMENT IN AAREY POWAI ROAD, GOREGAON EAST, MUMBAI
Bombay High Court
PIL No- 65 of 2017
Advocate on record

This Public Interest Litigation is filed for clearing garbage in entire Aarey Powai Order explaining as to how the environment are getting affected, as to how solid wastes lie in an area where there is a wide expanse of forest cover and the plants of varied species of fauna grow, this garbage/rubbish accumulation of solid waste has caused this forest soil which is known for its nature of adaptability to the soil cover of the trees that grow here has made the soil laterite or toxic in nature, which has destroyed the patch of green cover along this road. And as to how in the monsoons this garbage which flow to the drains make the drains chocked and cause flooding of contaminated water from the wastes and hence making it harmful for the people. After hearing this present matter, the Chief Justice of Bombay High Court on the same day passed an Order to clean Garbage in one month.

TO PAVE AND LEVEL THE ROAD WHICH IS FULL OF POTHOLES IN AAREY POWAI ROAD
Bombay High Court
PIL NO- 6 OF 2018
Advocate on record

This Public Interest Litigation regarding the Potholes on the entire stretch of Bangurda Village, Aarey Powai Road. In the said strech few thousand people reside along this stretch, and the road are used by the resident in the vicinity and also by the resident of the Royal Palm Complex.

The resident of the Royal Palm Complex use this road as an entry and exit, and other vehicle also use this road as a short cut to reach the highway of Goregaon. The said stretch of road was filled with potholes and the said road is driven by constant vehicular traffic from cars, trucks, water tankers, and is also a means for pedestrian. The said road that connects Aarey to Powai for which traversing through this road avoids the already existent congestion of the Aarey main road and hence also receives traffic from the people know to the said road.

Petition was filed explaining the dangers/risk to the life of people, children and animal around the place and the Government authority (BMC) were made party Petition was filed seeking a direction to BMC to level and pave the road. This Petition is pending in the Honorable High Court.
